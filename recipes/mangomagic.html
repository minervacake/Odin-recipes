Mango Magic Recipe

Description

Its very sweet, cool, and delicious!
Everyone should buy and try it out.
I swear, you wont regret it!

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Ice
  • Icecream
  • Grahams
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Blend ice, mango and milk.
  2. Put half in the cup and add crushed grahams.
  3. Add the rest of the shake in.
  4. Cut some mangoes and add them on top.
  5. Add a scoop of vanilla icecream on top.
  6. Get addictied
